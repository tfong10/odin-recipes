Lemon Grass Beef

This is a Vietnamese Lemon Grass Beef Dish

Ingredients

  • 1.5 lbs of thinly sliced beef
  • 1.5 tbsp of lemongrass powder
  • 1.5 tbsp of oyster sauce
  • 8 cloves of garlic
  • 1.5 tsp of chicken bouillon
  • 1 tsp of black pepper
  • 2 tsp of sugar
  • 3 tbsp of oil

Steps

  1. add in all ingredients and marinate for 10 minutes
  2. start stove top on high heat with oil
  3. cook beef for 5 mins
  4. serve on ricey
